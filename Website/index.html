AutoClicker

Auto is a free software which provides a variety of features to users. For instance, with the AutoClicker, you have the ability to set the position of the mouse cursor as well as the option to randomize it. In addition, you can choose from three click types; left click, right click, and middle click and you can decide how many clicks the program should execute before stopping. Moreover, you can set either a fixed delay or a random delay between clicks and assign a shortcut key which toggles the program.

AutoTyper

In addition to the AutoClicker, Auto features an AutoTyper which automatically types your specified message. The AutoTyper comes with a wide range of settings such as fixed or random delay between messages and control over capitalization. Furthermore, Auto gives you the option of pressing enter after typing your message as well as the ability to stop after executing a set amount of messages. Finally, Auto gives you the choice of either setting a custom shortcut key or clicking the "Start/Stop" button to toggle the AutoTyper.
